Research Compendia

The term "Research Compendium" was coined by Robert Gentleman and Duncan Temple Lang in their 2004 research paper. They re-imagined the scientific paper as bundles with the data and code used to generate the findings.

ResearchCompendia link data, code, and publication to provide a convenient way to understand, evaluate, and build on computational research.
